About Me

I’m Manav Patel, an end-to-end CAD designer and 3D printing technician. I bring your creative and industrial ideas to life using advanced modeling tools like Fusion 360 and SolidWorks, combined with hands-on 3D printing experience. My designs are functional, manufacturable, tested, and refined to support innovation across businesses, startups, and makerspaces.

How I Help Businesses

Design Optimization

I create 3D models that are engineered for performance, ease of printing, and cost-efficiency. Whether you need ergonomic tools or brand merch, I help refine ideas into ready-to-print designs.

Prototyping

Using my own 3D printer, I quickly iterate, test, and validate models to avoid costly manufacturing errors. Clients receive real-world results fast.

Production Support

I export files in STL, STEP, and OBJ formats with accurate tolerances — ready for CNC machining, injection molding, or FDM/Resin printing workflows.
